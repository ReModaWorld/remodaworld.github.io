FAQ

What’s the difference between E‑Design and Full Service?

E‑Design delivers a plan you implement yourself. Full Service includes purchasing, coordination, and on‑site styling.

Do you coordinate with contractors?

Yes. Our Design | Build model streamlines decisions, scheduling, and install details with your GC or our team.

How many revisions are included?

Each package includes a defined number (see cards). Additional revisions are available hourly.

Do you offer financing?

Yes—through Synchrony. We’ll review options at your consultation.
